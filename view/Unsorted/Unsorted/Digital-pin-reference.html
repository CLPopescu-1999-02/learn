Digital pin numbers for OEM devices

This lookup table provides a quick way to find the "Arduino" digital pin numbers used in firmware sketches, most of these designations are defined either by the internal makeup of the ATmega328P or by the device's hardware circuitry and components.

Digital pins 14 and up are ADC inputs that can be referenced by an analog pin number by subtracting 14
